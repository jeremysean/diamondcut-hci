Our History

Established in 1998

DiamondCut Motors was established in 1998 with a clear vision of becoming a prestigious name in the luxury car market in Indonesia. Founded by automotive enthusiasts with a passion for excellence, the company quickly gained recognition for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Delivering Luxury Brands

From its humble beginnings, DiamondCut Motors has grown exponentially, now offering a wide array of renowned luxury brands including Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others. Our dedication to cutting-edge service, timely delivery, and vehicle safety has attracted a loyal clientele, including successful business owners and discerning individuals who demand the best.

Our Passion

Over the years, DiamondCut Motors has not only expanded its product range but also its physical presence, with luxurious showrooms designed to provide a premium customer experience. As we look to the future, we remain focused on our goal of reaching more successful customers globally and pursuing massive expansion, ensuring that DiamondCut Motors continues to be a symbol of luxury and excellence in the automotive world.
